Historical :

In 1986 we conducted a demonstration of the choir " Music Hall " in the bandstand in the garden of Ranelagh in Avignon . Responsible for the Parks and Gardens of the city asked us to see and hear the prototype and despite a questionable version of a piece of Brahms , the test was successful. We were going to present the " Music Pavilion " for 7 years in 21 booths in the capital. This was the real starting point of our educational development. The first installation consisted of a grid 16 boxes that allow the public to play the music of our composition and works of the repertoire that we have specially adapted . The computerized control room was housed in the basement of the kiosk. The video camera that enabled interactivity was suspended , and the speakers above the space at the booth bulb center. A programmable clock put the system on a daily basis. No monitoring was necessary and the kiosk could still be used for orcherstres and brass bands. The sounds were produced by an integrated card to the Apple II .
