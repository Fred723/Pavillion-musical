Studies

Musical Pavilion provides music education from the age of 5 years, with no selection and no prior previous artistic practice. From the age of 5 years , students are welcomed during the musical awakening for a period of one year. Course Length awakening : 45 minutes. Children starting music from 6-7 years will be grouped with children of the same age groups.

  • Musical awakening - Frist degree
  • Second degree
  • Third degree

Courses may be offered during the year for students wishing to gain work experience. They are made in the partnership.

Internships available at this time are:

  • Current music & jazz : week of immersion in music full time in a chalet Queyras , to learn and grow while having fun.
  • Singing workshop polyphonic jazz bossa : PEZENAS (34) Saturday, January 31 and Sunday February 1 , 2015.

Free during testing shall be proposed for those still having doubts before enrolling . Two courses are given to help you make your choice.
